Full Coaching Report

After scoring, you’ll get a workplace + personal growth interpretation for your themes (3–5 paragraphs per theme).

How scoring works

Each theme gets an average from its Likert items. Forced-choice picks add a small bonus to the selected theme to reflect tradeoffs. Scores are ranked to produce your Top 5 and a full list.
